## Abstract 
<p align='justify'> I trained the YOLO_v2 on <a href="https://pjreddie.com/projects/pascal-voc-dataset-mirror/">PASCAL_VOC</a> dataset and then tested it on <a href="https://www.jbnu.ac.kr/kor/">CamVID</a> dataset.
